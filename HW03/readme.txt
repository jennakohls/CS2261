- press start to begin and to restart from lose/pause/win
- press select to pause
- press the left, right, up, and down arrows to move your player in all directions
- press A to fire a bullet. hit the asteroids with a bullet to clear them!
- press B to reverse gravity, making bullets move upside down
- if you are hit by an asteroid, you lose the game. if you hit all 5 asteroids, you win!
